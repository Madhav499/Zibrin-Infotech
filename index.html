14 / FAQ

Frequently
Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?+
Timelines vary by project complexity. A standard website: 4–8 weeks. Mobile apps: 8–20 weeks. Complex ERP/CRM systems: 12–32 weeks. We provide a precise, phased timeline after our discovery workshop, with fixed milestones and delivery dates you can plan around.
Do you offer post-launch maintenance?+
Yes. Every project includes 30 days of free post-launch support. Beyond that, we offer three maintenance plan tiers: Essential (security updates & monitoring), Growth (feature additions & optimisation), and Enterprise (dedicated engineer, SLA-backed support, priority response). We never abandon a product after launch.
Can you integrate AI into our existing systems?+
Absolutely. We specialise in retrofitting AI into existing products — adding GPT-powered chatbots, automating workflows, building recommendation engines, or implementing predictive analytics. We assess your existing stack, identify integration points, and implement AI capabilities without disrupting your current operations.
What is your project management approach?+
We use Agile methodology with 2-week sprints. Every client gets: a dedicated Project Manager, real-time access to our Jira project board, a private Slack channel, weekly video call demos of working software, and daily automated progress summaries. You're never in the dark about where your project stands.
How do you handle security and data privacy?+
Security is never an afterthought at Zibrin. Every project includes: SSL/TLS encryption, OWASP security guidelines, penetration testing, role-based access control, GDPR compliance architecture, encrypted database storage, and secure API authentication. We also conduct a security audit before every launch.
Do you work with international clients?+
Yes. We serve clients across India, the UK, USA, UAE, Canada, Australia, and Europe. We accommodate timezone-flexible communication, international payment methods (wire transfer, Wise, PayPal, crypto), cross-currency billing, and multilingual project documentation upon request.
What happens if the project goes over budget or timeline?+
We've never missed a delivery deadline in 8 years of operation. We provide fixed-price contracts for defined scope — so your budget is protected. For scope changes requested mid-project, we issue a clear change order with timeline and cost impact before any work begins. No surprises, ever.
Can you build and deploy on Play Store and App Store?+
Yes, completely. We handle the full deployment pipeline including: Google Developer Account setup, Apple Developer Program enrolment, store listing optimisation (ASO), screenshot design, app review submissions, and post-launch monitoring. We've deployed 80+ apps successfully across both platforms.
